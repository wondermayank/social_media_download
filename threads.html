How it works

01
Copy the link
Open the platform, find the post/video/reel, tap Share and copy the link.
02
Paste & choose
Paste the link in the box above and pick your download mode.
03
Hit Download
SaveIt calls your Cobalt instance and returns a direct download link instantly.
04
Save the file
Click the download button to save the file to your device.
