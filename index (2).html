Why SaveIt

Built different

No ads, no trackers
Powered by the open-source Cobalt engine. Zero tracking, no analytics, no cookies you didn't ask for.
No watermarks
TikTok videos, Instagram reels — downloaded clean, without platform watermarks or branding.
Instant downloads
No account needed. Paste the link, click download, get the file. That's the whole flow.
Multiple formats
Video, audio-only, original resolution — choose what you actually need per platform.
Self-hostable
Point SaveIt at your own Cobalt instance on Railway, Docker or any server for unlimited personal use.
Open source
Built on Cobalt (github.com/imputnet/cobalt) — 41k stars, AGPL-3.0 licensed, community-maintained.
